About

Hello beautiful people! We are excited to announce that we are switching from chemical based products to a natural and organic salon. We are the first and only establishment to use these healthy color lines and products. This has been my dream for a very long time. Everyday life is exposed to so many chemicals; I want you (my customers) to feel safe knowing we are here to change that. With ammonia and PPD free color it is long lasting with amazing grey coverage and the shine is just beautiful! Please feel free to call if you have any questions or concerns, we are happy to help!

Helping the environment while healing your hair!
